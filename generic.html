Hi, I'm Ava

Beyond technical expertise, I thrive in cross-functional collaborations, bridging the gap between data and decision-makers. My passion lies in uncovering hidden patterns, optimizing data models, and crafting solutions that not only solve complex problems but also shape the future of research and business.
